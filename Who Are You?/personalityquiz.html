Personality Quiz

Read each question carefully and answer honestly if you want your results to be accurate.
It's okay to skip a question if you don't know the answer.
Don't worry, it's completely anonymous!


  1. You are at a high school party and there is underage drinking going on. (Pretend you are underage if you aren't.) What would you do?







  2. It's the first day of school/work and you don't know anyone. What do you usually do?







  3. You see strangers gossiping about someone you don't know. What would you do?







  4. You hear an announcement at the mall that there is a lost little kid roaming around. What would you do?







  5. Your best friend has been talking to your worst enemy. What do you do?







  6. A classmate doesn't have lunch money. What do you do?







  7. What do you do after being rejected from something?







  8. What do you do when someone starts talking about something you strongly disagree with?







  9. You just met someone yesterday and you forgot their name. What do you do?







  10. How do you learn about new opportunities?
