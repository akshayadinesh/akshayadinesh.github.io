Career: Technology



     You should consider the possibility of a career in technology, IT, or computer science. People who work in this field typically possess an appreciation for detail, a knack for problem-solving and the ability to think analytically. If you're interested in learning more about a career in computer science and if it might be right for you, and conduct some research by consulting professionals in the field or a career counselor.
