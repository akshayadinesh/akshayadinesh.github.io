Career: Business



     You are best suited for careers in business, especially in leadership positions. Business administration is a wide field that incorporates many types of management positions. From major corporations to independent businesses, every operation needs skilled administrators in order to succeed. Motivated, organized people will thrive in business, where environments are often high-powered. Knowing how to deal with stress will help you keep your cool and keep your business in the black.
